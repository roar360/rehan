Common questions

Do I need to provide the content?

Basic content (your services, contact details, a few photos) helps, but I can help structure and write copy if you're not sure what to say.

What if I already have a domain?

I can deploy directly to your existing domain and host. If you don't have one yet, I'll recommend a cheap, reliable setup.

Will I be able to make edits later?

Yes. I hand over all files at the end. For simple text changes, I can teach you how to do it yourself in under 10 minutes.

What's the payment process?

50% upfront, 50% on delivery. UPI or bank transfer. No payment until we've agreed on the scope and price.
